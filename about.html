Who am I?

My name is Nick Lojas, I'm from Mississauga, Ontario, in Canada and as you may have read on the front page, I am a computer science graduate from the University of Toronto. I've been into programming ever since high school where I took my first computer science class learning python. Ever since, I've always known that I want to push the limits of what technology can do by creating and updating new and existing software on the market. I have many close friends that do a variety of programming with a few doing full stack web development, some doing front end web development, some developing software, and some developing games on unity which I've been eager to learn how to do.
On my own time apart from programming, I enjoy hitting the gym, hanging out with friends, going to music festivals, and p0wning n00bs in video games.
